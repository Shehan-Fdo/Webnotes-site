## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**AI in content workflows**

- AI helps you draft, outline, brainstorm, and research faster.
- It can also support daily tasks and improve productivity.
- Free or low-cost tools lower the barrier to getting started.
- AI works best as an aid, not a replacement for your skills.

**Drafting with AI**

- Use AI to generate first-pass ideas and rough structure.
- Keep the final message rooted in your own knowledge and judgment.
- Treat AI output as a starting point for revision.
- Avoid relying on complete drafts without review.

**Editing AI-generated content**

- Use AI to catch grammar and style issues.
- Use it to refine clarity and tighten wording.
- Check for factual errors before publishing.
- Watch for generic phrasing and weak originality.
- Add human detail to improve tone and depth.

**Quality and integrity**

- Human expertise remains the main driver of success.
- AI output can include hallucinations or inaccurate claims.
- Original thought should stay visible in the final piece.
- Do not sacrifice content integrity for speed.

**Ethical and publishing considerations**

- Disclose AI use when required by publishers or readers.
- Follow any applicable best practices for authors.
- Treat AI as a tool in the writing process, not an author substitute.
- Keep ownership, transparency, and trust in view.

**Practical workflow**

1. Use AI to research or outline the topic.
2. Draft the content with your own angle and examples.
3. Edit for grammar, clarity, and structure.
4. Verify facts and remove weak or repetitive language.
5. Review the final version for originality and disclosure needs.

**Wealth-building angle**

- AI can help you produce content faster.
- Faster production can support freelancing and content services.
- You can use AI to create monetizable creative work.
- Better workflow efficiency can improve output without large upfront costs.
