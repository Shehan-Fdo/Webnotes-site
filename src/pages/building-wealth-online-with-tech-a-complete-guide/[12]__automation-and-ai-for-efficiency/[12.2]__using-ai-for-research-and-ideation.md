## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Why AI helps with research and ideation**

- AI can reduce the time spent on research and idea generation.
- It helps you explore more options than manual brainstorming alone.
- It supports faster analysis of customer, market, and competitor information.
- It works best when you use it to assist, not replace, judgment.

**Where AI fits in the workflow**

- Use AI to collect and summarize information.
- Use AI to surface patterns, themes, and gaps.
- Use AI to generate first-pass ideas for content, products, or campaigns.
- Use human review to validate relevance and quality.

**Choosing practical use cases**

- Start with a clear business problem.
- Prioritize use cases that can create measurable value.
- Focus on tasks with high volume or repetitive effort.
- Avoid treating AI as the answer to every problem.
- Track effort against expected benefits.

**Research and ideation benefits**

- AI can speed up market research.
- It can support large-scale analysis of customer behavior.
- It can help you simulate scenarios and test assumptions.
- It can improve efficiency in content planning and idea discovery.
- It can free time for higher-level strategy.

**Best practices for using AI well**

- Prepare clean, organized input data.
- Give specific prompts and constraints.
- Review outputs for accuracy and usefulness.
- Keep a human in the loop for final decisions.
- Start small and expand after you prove value.

**Risk and quality controls**

- Use content guardrails to protect quality and brand consistency.
- Check outputs for compliance and privacy concerns.
- Be cautious with regulated financial services use cases.
- Verify important claims before you act on them.
- Watch for misinformation and overconfident outputs.

**Common pitfalls to avoid**

- Do not assume AI output is automatically correct.
- Do not skip validation because the process is faster.
- Do not launch broad use cases without a clear objective.
- Do not ignore regulatory and policy requirements.
- Do not scale before you test the workflow.

**Practical approach to ideation**

1. Define the problem you want to solve.
2. Ask AI for multiple angles, themes, or concepts.
3. Compare the ideas against your goals and constraints.
4. Refine the strongest options with human input.
5. Test the best idea in a small, low-risk way.

**What to remember**

- AI is strongest as a research and brainstorming partner.
- Speed and scale are major advantages.
- Human judgment remains essential for strategy and risk.
- The best results come from focused use cases and careful review.
