## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Why automate lead capture and follow-up**

- Manual lead generation is slow, error-prone, and hard to scale.
- Automation helps you capture, qualify, and nurture leads 24/7.
- Faster response times improve the chance that you reach leads first.
- AI can reduce busywork and free time for higher-value sales tasks.
- Personalized outreach supports better engagement than generic blasts.

**What automation can handle**

- **Lead capture** from forms, landing pages, and other entry points.
- **Lead scoring** to prioritize high-intent prospects.
- **CRM** updates so records stay current without manual entry.
- **Email sequences** that send follow-up based on behavior or timing.
- **Task routing** so next steps reach the right person.

**How to build the workflow**

1. Define the lead source and the action you want after capture.
2. Connect the capture point to your CRM or email platform.
3. Add rules for scoring, tagging, or segmenting leads.
4. Create a follow-up sequence with timely, relevant messages.
5. Test the workflow and confirm each step triggers correctly.
6. Review results and adjust timing, messaging, or routing.

**AI use cases in the process**

- AI can help analyze datasets to find high-intent buyers.
- AI can support faster response times by automating routine actions.
- AI can draft and refine outreach content for different audiences.
- AI can assist with market research and trend analysis.
- AI can summarize meetings and assign action items after calls.

**Follow-up best practices**

- Send messages based on customer behavior and interests.
- Keep the sequence focused on the next useful action.
- Use personalized content instead of broad email blasts.
- Measure conversion rate and cost per customer, not only opens.
- Keep the system simple enough to maintain and improve.

**Common workflows to automate**

- Welcome sequences for new leads.
- Drip campaigns for ongoing nurture.
- Lead qualification based on engagement signals.
- Content follow-up after downloads or inquiries.
- Sales handoff when a lead shows strong intent.

**Practical benefits**

- More leads move through the funnel with less manual work.
- Teams spend less time on repetitive follow-up.
- Messaging stays more consistent across touchpoints.
- Revenue processes become easier to scale.
- You reduce the risk of missed opportunities.

> Use conservative automation first. Overly complex workflows can be hard to set up and may fail to deliver a clear return on investment.
