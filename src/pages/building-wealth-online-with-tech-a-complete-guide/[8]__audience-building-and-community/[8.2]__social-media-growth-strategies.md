## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Social media growth basics**

- Social media marketing works best with a clear plan, not random posting.
- It combines **messaging**, timing, creativity, and analytics.
- You grow by showing up where your audience already is.
- Use platforms such as `Facebook`, `Instagram`, `X`, and `LinkedIn` to share content and engage followers.
- The goal is to build visibility, trust, and real results.

**Audience and community**

- Focus on **engagement**, not just likes.
- Reply to comments to make followers feel seen.
- Comment on other people’s content to build connection.
- Share useful content that people want or need.
- Let your personality stand out so people remember you.
- Build community through content before a sale happens.
- Create evergreen content that keeps working over time.

**Strategy and planning**

- Start with a purposeful strategy that aligns with your brand.
- Set realistic growth goals before you scale activity.
- Use competitor analysis to understand your position.
- Match your content and tactics to your audience and platform.
- Avoid chasing follower counts as the only success measure.
- Treat growth as a mix of visibility, engagement, and business impact.

**Metrics and measurement**

- Track **metrics** to see whether your content supports your goals.
- Use a mix of data points instead of one “magic” number.
- Measure likes, clicks, views, and engagement to understand response.
- Use metrics to learn what content resonates with your audience.
- Review performance regularly so you can refine your strategy.
- Look for signals that show business value, not just attention.

**Practical growth focus**

- Create content that cuts through noise and adds value.
- Use targeted ads when they support your plan.
- Keep your message consistent across posts and platforms.
- Build lasting engagement instead of short-term vanity results.
- Adjust your approach based on what the data shows.
