## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Why an email list matters**

- An email list gives you a direct line to your audience.
- You own the list, unlike social media followers or search traffic.
- Email marketing helps you nurture leads and convert them into customers.
- A strong list supports trust, product launches, and consistent sales.
- Quality matters more than list size for engagement and conversions.

**Opt-in basics**

- Use **opt-in** marketing to get verifiable permission before sending messages.
- Send email only to people who willingly provide their address.
- Avoid unsolicited bulk email.
- A consent-based list usually improves opens, clicks, and sales.
- An opt-in list can include email or text message subscribers who explicitly agree to hear from you.

**Ways to grow your list**

- Add opt-in signup forms to your website.
- Use a **lead magnet** to encourage visitors to share their contact details.
- Offer useful content that helps people enter your sales funnel.
- Use popup forms to collect email addresses while visitors browse.
- Combine forms with other list-building tactics when appropriate.

**Lead magnets and forms**

- A lead magnet is a resource or offer that trades value for contact information.
- Lead magnets help turn website visitors into leads.
- Popup forms can collect email addresses, phone numbers, and other details.
- Keep forms easy to find and simple to complete.
- Match the offer to the audience’s interest to improve signups.

**Practical list-building focus**

- Prioritize subscribers who want your content.
- Track engagement, not just subscriber count.
- Use your list to share your story and promote your products.
- Treat the list as a long-term business asset.
- Build on a channel you control instead of relying only on platform algorithms.
