## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Copywriting and funnels**

- Copywriting helps move a reader from awareness to action.
- A **sales funnel** is a step-by-step path from “I’ve never heard of you” to “I just bought from you.”
- A **conversion funnel** guides consumers through the buying journey so they reach the intended outcome.
- Good funnel copy does more than fill pages.
- It supports the customer journey with clear messaging, offers, and next steps.
- Small copy mistakes can hurt conversions.
- Weak headlines, missing tags, or broken follow-up logic can reduce results.

**Core copywriting principles**

- Start with the audience and the product.
- Organize the message for maximum impact.
- Use structure to reduce guesswork.
- Treat formulas as thinking tools, not just acronyms.
- Match the formula to the audience’s awareness level.
- Use the right framework for the job instead of forcing one style everywhere.

**Copywriting formulas**

- Copywriting formulas help you write faster.
- They give you a starting point for pages, emails, ads, and posts.
- They help you face the blank page with more confidence.
- They can also help you spot weak sections in a headline, button, or script.
- Common formulas like **AIDA** and **PAS** work because they structure persuasion.
- These formulas map to how people process information and emotion.
- Use formulas as a guide, then adapt them to the message.

**Features and benefits**

- Features describe what a product is.
- Benefits describe what the product does for the customer.
- Features use factual, specific language.
- Benefits use solution-oriented language.
- Features build trust with facts.
- Benefits connect to customer outcomes and practical value.
- Strong copy balances both.

**Calls to action**

- A **call to action** tells the reader what to do next.
- Effective CTAs give a reason to act.
- They may offer a free resource, a special perk, or unique access.
- CTAs guide potential customers through the buying journey.
- Clear CTA language supports conversion.

**Practical writing focus**

- Write copy that converts cold traffic.
- Keep the customer journey in view.
- Use headlines, offers, and CTAs to move the reader forward.
- Test and refine copy when possible.
- Improve the message before assuming the traffic is the problem.
