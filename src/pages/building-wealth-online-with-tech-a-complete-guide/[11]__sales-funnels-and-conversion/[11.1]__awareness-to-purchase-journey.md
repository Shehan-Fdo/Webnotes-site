## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Journey overview**

- A **sales funnel** guides someone from never hearing about you to buying from you.
- A **conversion funnel** helps you understand how people move through your site or product.
- The journey works best as a clear path, not a collection of disconnected tactics.
- For ecommerce, the funnel often maps from awareness to retention and loyalty.

**Core stages**

- **Awareness** is the first point of discovery.
- **Interest** captures attention and sparks curiosity.
- **Consideration** helps people compare options.
- **Decision** supports the choice to buy.
- **Conversion** is the purchase moment.
- **Retention** turns buyers into repeat customers.

**What each stage should do**

- Awareness should get you noticed in a crowded market.
- Interest should hold attention long enough to move people forward.
- Consideration should reduce uncertainty.
- Decision should make the choice feel clear.
- Conversion should feel smooth and secure.
- Retention should support repeat purchases and loyalty.

**Why funnels matter**

- Funnels show how effective your marketing is.
- Funnels help you spot problems in the customer path.
- Funnels support better sales decisions.
- Funnels can improve website efficiency and ROI.
- Funnels give you a roadmap for growth.

**Building the path**

- Map content to each stage of the funnel.
- Match your message to the visitor’s level of awareness.
- Remove friction where people hesitate.
- Keep the buying path simple.
- Use the funnel to guide people step by step.

**Optimization focus**

- Track where people drop off.
- Test changes to improve performance.
- Use automation where it reduces manual work.
- Review the funnel continuously.
- Improve the experience at each stage, not only at purchase.

**Practical takeaway**

- Start with a simple funnel if you are early in the process.
- Focus on moving people from first contact to first purchase.
- Treat the journey as a system you can refine over time.
- Use the funnel to build a repeatable path to revenue.
