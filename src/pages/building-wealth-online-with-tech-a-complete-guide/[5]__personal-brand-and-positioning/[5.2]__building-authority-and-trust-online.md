## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Authority and trust basics**

- **Authority** is not fame.
- It comes from being seen as credible and useful.
- Personal branding helps you communicate your value proposition.
- Your brand is the way you present yourself online and how others perceive you.
- It reflects your skills, experience, and personality.
- A clear brand helps you stand out in a competitive market.

**Why trust matters**

- **Brand trust** is confidence that you will follow through on your promises.
- Trust affects whether people buy, return, or recommend you.
- Losing trust can damage future opportunities.
- A trusted brand can support higher willingness to pay.
- Trust grows from consistent experiences and public signals.

**What builds a strong personal brand**

- Share your skills, values, and voice clearly.
- Show what makes you distinct and authentic.
- Use content, social media, and interactions intentionally.
- Keep your message aligned across posts, articles, and comments.
- Focus on usefulness over self-promotion.
- Build a brand that works for you over time.

**Using social media well**

- Social media can increase visibility.
- It can also become a distraction if you chase performance.
- Avoid turning yourself into a caricature of your business.
- Use platforms to reinforce your expertise.
- Treat each post as part of your digital reputation.
- Aim for influence, not just attention.

**Practical positioning**

- Present a clear personal value proposition.
- Show the difference you want to make.
- Use your human edge to create relatability.
- Build credibility through steady, intentional communication.
- Let your online presence compound over time.
- Focus on being memorable for the right reasons.
