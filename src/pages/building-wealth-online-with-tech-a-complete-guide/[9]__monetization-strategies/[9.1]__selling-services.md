## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Service businesses**

- Service businesses are a fast path to online income because you sell your time and skills directly.
- Common service offers include writing, design, consulting, bookkeeping, and virtual assistance.
- You do not need inventory, startup capital, or complex systems to begin.
- Profit margins can be high because your main costs are your time and basic software subscriptions.
- Treat the work like a real business, not a side hobby.

**Finding a sellable service**

- Focus on ordinary problems for specific groups of people.
- A strong service offer solves a clear need consistently.
- You do not need a groundbreaking idea to make money online.
- Specificity helps you match your skills to a buyer’s problem.

**Pricing and packaging**

- Package services so they are simple and clear.
- Avoid overcomplicated offers that confuse buyers.
- Pricing and packaging shape who buys and whether they stick around.
- Consider billing in advance when your service model supports it.
- Define what is included in each package before you sell it.

**Practical selling approach**

1. Identify a skill you can deliver reliably.
2. Choose a specific audience with a clear problem.
3. Create a simple service package.
4. Set a price that reflects your time and costs.
5. Sell the offer consistently and improve it from feedback.

**Key takeaways**

- Service work can produce income quickly compared with more complex online models.
- Clear offers are easier to sell than vague or overloaded ones.
- Consistent delivery matters as much as the initial sale.
- Simple packaging supports better client understanding and smoother sales.
