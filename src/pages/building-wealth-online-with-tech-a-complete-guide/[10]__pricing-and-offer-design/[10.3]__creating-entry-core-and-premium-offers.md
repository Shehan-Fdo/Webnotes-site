## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Offer ladder basics**

- Build offers as a **value ladder**.
- Start with a low-risk entry offer.
- Move customers toward higher-value offers over time.
- Use each step to deepen trust before a bigger commitment.
- Avoid asking for a premium purchase on first contact.

**Entry offers**

- Keep entry offers small and easy to buy.
- Use them to turn interest into a first transaction.
- A **tripwire** is a low-cost offer designed to convert an audience member into a buyer.
- Keep the promise clear and the purchase simple.
- Use entry offers to qualify buyers, not to solve everything.

**Core offers**

- Make the core offer your main solution.
- Use it to address the central problem your audience wants solved.
- Keep the offer focused instead of trying to sell everything at once.
- A single funnel can support multiple exit points.
- Optimize the path toward booked calls or the next logical step.

**Premium offers**

- Reserve premium offers for higher trust and higher commitment.
- Use them after the audience has seen value from earlier steps.
- Premium services can include direct mentorship or done-for-you support.
- Position premium offers as the next level in the relationship.
- Keep the path to premium gradual and intentional.

**Pricing and value**

- Use **value-based pricing** when customer perception drives price.
- Price around the value customers believe they receive.
- Focus on the difference between appreciation and payment.
- Align price with the outcome, not just the cost to deliver.
- Use pricing to support profit without making the offer feel disconnected from value.

**Practical structure**

- Map offers from small to large.
- Make each step feel like a natural next move.
- Keep the ladder simple enough to understand quickly.
- Use one offer to create momentum for the next.
- Build for trust, repeat buying, and clear progression.

**Key takeaways**

- Wealth-building businesses often rely on scalable, repeatable offers.
- Small offers can start the relationship.
- Core offers should solve the main problem.
- Premium offers should reward trust with deeper access or support.
- A clear ladder helps customers move forward without friction.
