## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Ethics and mindset**

- Wealth-building starts with **strategic thinking**.
- Your decisions shape your financial outcomes.
- Self-examination supports better judgment.
- Improve health, knowledge, and skills to strengthen your habits.
- Hard work helps make improvement routine.
- A strong mindset supports both earning and keeping wealth.

**Legal compliance in online business**

- Online business follows core business law principles.
- Digital operations add extra concerns.
- Focus on consumer protection, privacy, and international trade.
- Stay compliant to reduce legal risk.
- Treat legal awareness as part of business planning.

**Privacy and data handling**

- Privacy rules matter for online businesses.
- Customer data can trigger compliance obligations.
- Review how tools and integrations collect information.
- Protect personal data with clear handling practices.
- Assume privacy expectations apply even in small businesses.

**Intellectual property**

- **Intellectual property** includes code, branding, designs, and workflows.
- Protect valuable assets with registration or confidentiality measures when needed.
- Document ownership and access controls.
- Unprotected ideas are easier to copy.
- Legal value depends on enforceable rights.

**Long-term thinking**

- Build for durability, not quick wins.
- Use interdisciplinary thinking to stay flexible.
- Broad knowledge supports better adaptation.
- Long-term success depends on consistent improvement.
- Strategic planning helps you adjust as conditions change.

**Practical guardrails**

- Check legal requirements before launching offers.
- Keep records for ownership, permissions, and compliance.
- Limit access to sensitive assets.
- Review privacy and protection practices regularly.
- Make decisions that support future stability.
