## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Technology and wealth creation**

- Technology acts as a **catalyst** for economic transformation.
- It reshapes how individuals and businesses generate wealth.
- Digital tools make wealth creation more accessible, scalable, and diverse.
- Technology changes traditional industries and creates new sectors.
- Financial technology, or **fintech**, is central to this shift.

**How fintech changes wealth management**

- Fintech reshapes how investors interact with portfolios.
- It changes how investors receive advice.
- It changes how investors make decisions about wealth.
- Automated investment platforms improve efficiency and accessibility.
- These platforms can make financial advice more available to smaller portfolios.

**Robo-advisors and automation**

- **Robo-advisors** use algorithms to manage investments.
- They offer a low-cost alternative to traditional advisory services.
- They can handle portfolio rebalancing.
- They can support tax-loss harvesting.

**AI and data-driven investing**

- **AI** (artificial intelligence) supports more context-aware investing.
- Advanced analytics help make smarter portfolio strategies more accessible.
- AI can improve personalization for goal-driven investment journeys.
- Responsible AI use depends on trust, transparency, and explainability.

**Benefits for investors**

- Technology streamlines wealth management processes.
- It improves transparency in financial services.
- It expands access to advice and investing tools.
- It supports more tailored investment experiences.
- It helps investors make faster, more informed decisions.

**Practical takeaways**

- Use technology to reduce manual work in investing workflows.
- Choose tools that match your portfolio size and goals.
- Prefer platforms that explain decisions clearly.
- Treat automation as support, not a replacement for judgment.
- Watch for tools that improve efficiency without reducing transparency.
