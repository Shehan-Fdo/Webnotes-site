## **Course:** CompTia Security Plus

**Risk management overview**

- Risk management helps you identify, evaluate, and reduce risk.
- **GRC** means governance, risk, and compliance.
- GRC aligns IT and business objectives.
- GRC helps you manage risk while supporting business operations.
- GRC also supports adherence to applicable laws and requirements.

**Risk and impact**

- Risk is the chance that something does not work as planned.
- Impact is the cost or effect when a risk happens.
- Impact can include injury or loss of life.
- Impact can include property damage.
- Impact can include environmental damage.
- Impact can include financial loss.

**Business impact analysis**

- **Business impact analysis (BIA)** identifies the source and relative impact of risk elements in a process.
- BIA shows how loss of a critical function affects the organization.
- Use BIA to understand what matters most during an outage.
- Use BIA to support recovery planning.

**Risk assessment**

- Risk assessment evaluates threats and their likely impact.
- Threats can come from the environment.
- Threats can include severe weather such as tornadoes, hurricanes, and earthquakes.
- Threats can be internal.
- Threats can be external.
- Internal threats often come from employees.
- External threats come from outside the organization.

**Quantitative risk terms**

- **Annualized rate of occurrence (ARO)** is the expected number of times a threat occurs in a year.
- Use ARO to estimate how often a threat may affect you.
- **Single loss expectancy (SLE)** is the loss from one event.
- **Annualized loss expectancy (ALE)** is the expected yearly loss from a threat.
- Use these terms when you need numeric risk analysis.

**Risk management goals**

- Risk management helps organizations maximize return on investment.
- Risk management supports better decisions about security spending.
- Risk management helps you focus on the most important risks first.

**Practical study focus**

- Know the difference between risk and impact.
- Know how BIA supports continuity planning.
- Know the difference between internal and external threats.
- Know the basic meaning of ARO, SLE, and ALE.
- Know that GRC connects security work to business and compliance needs.
