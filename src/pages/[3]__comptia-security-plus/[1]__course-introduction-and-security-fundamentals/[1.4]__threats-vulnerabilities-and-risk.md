## **Course:** CompTia Security Plus

**Threats, vulnerabilities, and risk**

- Security+ focuses on identifying **threats**, attacks, and vulnerabilities.
- You use practical skills to protect networks, applications, and devices.
- Cybersecurity supports confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
- Security measures help prevent security incidents, data breaches, and loss of critical systems.

**Threats and attacks**

- Threats include malicious activity that targets systems or data.
- Malware is malicious software that can damage systems.
- Malware can also steal sensitive data or hold systems for ransom.
- Social engineering uses deception to influence people.
- Application attacks target software and its weaknesses.

**Vulnerabilities**

- A vulnerability is a weakness that can be exploited.
- Vulnerabilities can exist in systems, applications, or processes.
- Attackers often combine a vulnerability with a threat to cause harm.
- Reducing vulnerabilities lowers exposure to attack.

**Risk management**

- Security+ includes assessing and managing **risk**.
- Risk analysis helps you understand what could happen and how likely it is.
- Mitigation strategies reduce the impact or likelihood of harm.
- Business continuity planning helps you keep operations running during disruption.

**Security focus areas**

- Firewalls help control network traffic.
- Intrusion detection systems help identify suspicious activity.
- Endpoint security helps protect devices.
- Secure protocols and secure network design support stronger architecture.
- Identity and access concepts help control who can access resources.

**Study focus**

- Learn common threat types and how they affect systems.
- Connect vulnerabilities to the attacks that exploit them.
- Practice matching controls to the risk they reduce.
- Review how security supports real-world operations.
