## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Affiliate marketing basics**

- Affiliate marketing is a **revenue-sharing** model.
- You promote a merchant’s product or service.
- You earn a commission for a sale or lead.
- The model is performance-based.
- It can support passive income and long-term wealth building.

**How the partnership works**

- The main actors are the affiliate, the merchant, the affiliate network, and the customer.
- The affiliate drives traffic or referrals.
- The merchant provides the product or service.
- The network may connect affiliates and merchants.
- The customer completes the purchase or lead action.

**Getting started**

- Choose a niche with clear demand.
- Focus on ordinary problems for a specific audience.
- Select profitable affiliate programs.
- Build content that matches buyer intent.
- Drive targeted traffic to your offers.
- Track conversions and refine what works.

**Trust and conversions**

- Treat the work like a real business.
- Use niche targeting to stay relevant.
- Build trust before asking for a click.
- Keep recommendations aligned with audience needs.
- Optimize for conversions, not just traffic.

**Disclosure and compliance**

- Use an **affiliate disclosure** when you receive compensation.
- Place the disclosure near affiliate links or endorsements.
- Write it in clear, simple language.
- Make it noticeable and easy to understand.
- Follow FTC (Federal Trade Commission) requirements in the U.S.

> If you earn a commission from a link, disclose it clearly and prominently.

**Practical business notes**

- Affiliate marketing has a low barrier to entry.
- It can work as a side income or a full-time business.
- Some large programs offer broad reach and commission-based earnings.
- Success depends on consistency, relevance, and audience trust.

**Common focus areas**

- Content creation
- Website optimization
- Targeted traffic
- Conversion improvement
- Partner selection
