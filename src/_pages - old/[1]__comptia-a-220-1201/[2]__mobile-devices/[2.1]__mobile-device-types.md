## **Course:** Comptia A+ 220-1201

**Mobile Device Categories**

Mobile devices include several common form factors that support communication, media, and portable computing.

- **Tablets** usually run `iOS` or `Android`.
- Tablets typically use a touchscreen and may support a stylus.
- Tablets are usually about `7 inches` diagonal or larger.
- Tablets support productivity, games, and media playback.
- **Smartphones** are compact communication devices.
- Smartphones support calls, text, email, and media viewing.
- Smartphones usually range from about `3.5 inches` to `6 inches` diagonal.
- **Wearable technology** includes devices you wear on the body.
- Wearables often extend mobile access without a traditional handheld form factor.
- **E-readers** focus on reading digital books and similar content.
- E-readers are a separate mobile device category from general-purpose tablets.

**Tablets**

Tablets are a major mobile device type for touch-based computing.

- You use your finger or a stylus for input.
- Some tablets show a virtual keyboard on screen.
- Tablets often support audio and video media.
- Tablets can run many application types.

**Smartphones**

Smartphones act as a mobile communication hub.

- You use them for voice communication.
- You use them for messaging and email.
- You can also use them for video conferencing.
- Smartphones are also useful as media viewers.

**Mobile Device Use Cases**

Different device types fit different user needs.

- Use a tablet when you want a larger touch interface.
- Use a smartphone when you need a highly portable communication device.
- Use a wearable when you want hands-free or body-worn access.
- Use an e-reader when you want a reading-focused device.

**Exam Focus**

CompTIA A+ Core 1 expects you to recognize common mobile device types and their general uses.

- Know the basic form factor of each device type.
- Know the primary purpose of each device type.
- Know that tablets and smartphones often use touch input.
- Know that mobile devices support communication and media in different ways.
