## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Content format basics**

- Content creation means creating and publishing content online.
- It helps you capture attention.
- It supports brand visibility.
- It can help grow revenue.
- A content strategy is the higher-level plan for how you craft, distribute, and promote content.
- Strategy helps you create repeatable content that supports business goals.
- Random content creation is not enough.

**Video format**

- Video is often a high-performing format across platforms.
- Short-form vertical video can improve discoverability on platforms like TikTok and Instagram.
- Use video when you want fast attention and broad reach.
- Video works well when your audience prefers quick, visual learning.

**Audio format**

- Podcasts give you another way to reach people who prefer listening.
- Audio can fit audiences that consume content while multitasking.
- Choose audio when your topic works well in a spoken, conversational format.
- Audio should still match your audience and goals.

**Written format**

- Written content includes blog posts, articles, white papers, ebooks, and newsletters.
- Blog posts are flexible for industry insights, how-to education, listicles, and timely updates.
- Written content supports search visibility and evergreen traffic.
- Detailed guides and case studies work well in written form.
- Many audiences still prefer learning from blog content over traditional advertising.

**Choosing the right format**

- Start with your audience.
- Match the format to your goals.
- Use keyword research to guide topics.
- Pick formats that fit how people already consume content.
- Combine formats when one asset can support several channels.
- Use long-lasting content for compounding value.
- Use short-run content for timely reach.

**Practical planning**

1. Define your content goal.
2. Identify your core audience.
3. Research the formats they already use.
4. Choose the format that best fits the message.
5. Publish and review performance.
6. Adjust based on what resonates.

**Common guidance**

- Do not rely on random posting.
- Do not ignore audience preferences.
- Do not treat one format as best for every situation.
- Do not skip original insight in written content.
- Do not assume content works without a plan.

**Key takeaway**

- Video, audio, and written content each serve different needs.
- The best choice depends on audience, goals, and distribution.
- Strong content strategy uses the right format for the right job.
