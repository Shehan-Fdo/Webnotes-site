## **Course:** Building Wealth Online with Tech: A Complete Guide

**Content strategy basics**

- **Content strategy** is the higher-level plan for how you craft, distribute, and promote content.
- It can also support audience engagement, traffic, leads, and other business goals.
- A strong strategy makes content more measurable, repeatable, and iterative.
- It should reflect what people want, not just what you want to publish.
- Without a strategy, you do not know what your audience wants or how to meet that need.

**Content pillars**

- **Content pillars** give your content structure and focus.
- They help you stay centered on the problem areas your brand can solve.
- They also help you speak to topics that matter to your niche audience.
- In saturated channels, structure helps prevent fragmented messaging.
- Pillars make it easier to create recognition instead of random output.

**Choosing pillars**

- Start with your niche and ideal customer profile, or **ICP**.
- Map your skills to the topics you can credibly cover.
- Include topics you care about and can sustain over time.
- Use audience feedback to learn what people struggle with.
- Choose pillars that align with both expertise and audience interest.

**Editorial planning**

- Turn pillars into a repeatable content plan.
- Use the pillars to guide what you publish and when you publish it.
- Build content around a clear purpose instead of reacting post by post.
- Keep the plan aligned with business goals.
- Use the plan to support consistent growth and stronger audience trust.

**Practical workflow**

1. Define your niche and ICP.
2. List your skills and interests.
3. Talk about those topics consistently.
4. Engage with people who respond to your content.
5. Ask what problems they want solved.
6. Refine your pillars based on what you learn.

**Why this matters**

- A clear plan helps you create content with direction and structure.
- It supports authority by showing consistent expertise.
- It helps build trust through useful, relevant content.
- It can improve the path from attention to leads and revenue.
- It makes your content system easier to scale.
